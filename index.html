About Me

My name is Chih-Mei Young. I was born and raised in Taichung, Taiwan. I have accomplished my bachelor’s degree study in 2018, master’s degree study in 2020 and now pursuing doctoral study in Chemical Engineering at National Tsing Hua University, Hsinchu, Taiwan.

Self-expectation

As starting up my own business and inventing helpful products or even opening up a new industry that will benefit the society has been one of my long-term future plans, I have been endeavoring to consolidate the fundamentals and to strengthen my research competencies, which would enable me to exploit rigorous chemical engineering skills, concepts, and knowledge more cleverly to achieve this goal.
